We Love Alumni!

Endowment

The Scotch’n’Soda Endowment Fund is a fund that was set up by an anonymous donor a few years ago. Every quarter interest from the fund, based on the interest/income rate set by the University, is deposited into our account. This fund is used exclusively for large, show-specific purchases.

Donating

Donating can be made many different ways:
  • Via check made out to Scotch’n’Soda Theatre or check made out to Carnegie Mellon University Student Activities with a designation to Scotch’n’Soda.

    Please send to:
    Carnegie Mellon University
    P.O. Box 371525
    Pittsburgh, PA 15251-7525

  • Online here - follow on-screen instructions
  • Over the phone at 412-268-2021
  • Or, via wire transfer. More information on that here.
You can also donate directly to SnS when contacted by a student caller. When they ask for a donation, just tell them that you’d like to send the money to Scotch’n’Soda Theatre and they’ll make it happen.

Thanks so much for your donation!
